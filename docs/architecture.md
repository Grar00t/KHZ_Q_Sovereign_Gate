## Key Algorithms

### QAOA (Quantum Approximate Optimization Algorithm)
Classical simulation mapping ethical decisions to an Ising cost Hamiltonian. Alternating cost/mixer layers approximate optimal ethical configurations preserving Nasl coherence.

### VQE (Variational Quantum Eigensolver)
Variational ground-state solver. Minimizes expectation value of the ethical Hamiltonian to find lowest-energy (most Fitrah-aligned) decision state.

### SVD Ethical Prism
Singular Value Decomposition of decision matrices. Decomposes ethical influence into principal components — separating Fitrah-aligned signal from bias noise.

### Tensor Networks (MPS)
Matrix Product State simulation for compressing high-dimensional ethical state spaces. Entanglement entropy measures coherence of ethical decision chains.
